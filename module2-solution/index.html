An Ode to Meatballs and Love Lost

And here's the title
This would be my first box! On top of spaghetti all covered with cheese, I lost my poor meatball when somebody sneezed, It rolled off the table, it rolled on the floor, And then my poor meatball rolled out of the door.
Anotha One
This would be my second box! It rolled in the garden and under a bush, And then my poor meatball was nothing but mush.The mush was as tasty as tasty could be, And early next summer it grew to a tree.
ANOTHA ONE
This would be my third box!The tree was all covered with beautiful moss, It grew great big meatballs and tomato sauce.So if you eat spaghetti all covered with cheese, Hold on to your meatball and don't ever sneeze.
